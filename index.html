Aria of Sorrow

An origin settles into place.

Continuation is assumed.

—

Some endings arrive without ceremony.

What is remembered shapes what follows.

Every outcome begins somewhere unseen.

Certain choices cannot be undone.

What remains tends to endure quietly.

Systems keep their own records.

Sound fades, but echoes do not.

Unchecked structures slowly break down.

Patterns repeat even when denied.

Adjustment usually comes too late.

Effort produces what it can, nothing more.

The same mistake often appears again.

It appears again, unchanged.

What is sent out eventually comes back.

It always comes back.

—
